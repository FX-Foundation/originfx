# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in FX Foundation a welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of background, experience level, or identity.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Give and accept feedback graciously
- Focus on what is best for the community and the project
- Show empathy toward other community members

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks of any kind
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior
- Sustained off-topic derailing of discussions
- Any conduct that a reasonable person would consider inappropriate

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported to the maintainers. Reports will be reviewed and addressed. Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this code, and to temporarily or permanently ban contributors for behavior deemed harmful.
